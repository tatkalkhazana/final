What is Tatkal Software ?

Tatkal software is a type of software designed to help people book tickets through the I Railways' Tatkal quota. Tatkal quota is a special quota that allows passengers to book their train tickets at a higher fare than the regular booking fare. This is meant to provide last-minute bookings for people who have urgent travel needs. However, the Tatkal booking process can be quite challenging, and tickets often get sold out within minutes of the quota opening.
